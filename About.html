ABOUT US

organization, details, logistics


Three words that can spoil any bride or chair person’s special day. As a boutique company, we’re driven by a childlike excitement for what we do and a love of collaboration with our clients. It’s our goal to leave you and your guests with an event that exceeds expectations and lasts in your memories long after the last toast.
